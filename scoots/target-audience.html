Target Audience

The target audience of scoots:

    Who: Men & women, single, or married.
    Age: 25-55
    Income: Income levels will typicaly be between $60,000 to $200,000.
    Education: Collage degrees.

Scenerios:

  1. Couple looking from a ported cruise ship looking for a way to see the mayan ruins.
  2. Group of friends looking for a way to explore the jungle on Cozumel.
  3. What trail's can I take my ATV on?
  4. Single looking for something small to get him place to place on Cozumel.
  5. Family of 5 looking for something bigger than an ATV so they can explore the island but be able to go off road too.

Persona 1

Isabel and George are engaged. They are planning their honeymoon to Cozumel, Mexico. They are excited for beach time but enjoy a good adventure. They are looking for ways to see the sites but have a good time while doing it. They enjoy the convenience of how small scooters are and how easy they are to park. So they are looking to rent scooters to get them around the island.

Persona 2

Liam recently graduated from college in computer engineering. He recently landed a well paying job. He is celebrating his success with a group of his three fellow graduates with a cruise that ports in Cozumel for 2 days. He and his buddies have just arrived on the island are looking for fun things to do around the island for the next couple days. With a quick Google search they found scoots and are now trying to figure out if they want to rent and jeep or a couple of ATVs. They are super excited because they found so many great options. Top of their list is the Mayan ruins, Zona Arqueologica San Gervasio.
