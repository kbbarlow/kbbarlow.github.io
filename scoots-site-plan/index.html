Site Purpose

Scoots purpose is to rent motor scooters, ATV’s, and jeeps to tourist, locals, and walk-ins on the Island of Cozumel. The site’s purpose is to show customers what is available to rent and give all the information needed to make an educated decision. The site will walk through the rentals process and the rental requirements.

The website needs to be attractive, fast loading, and responsive to make the transaction quick and painless.

The website will give local information so that customers are in the know about the island. It will have local weather, trails and roads to take the rentals on, and possibly local law’s with the rentals.
