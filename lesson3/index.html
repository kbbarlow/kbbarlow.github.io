Site Purpose

Southeast Idaho Local is the place to go when you want to find out what's going on in the area. The website has events. It has fun outdoor activity ideas, including hikes in the area, mountain biking, places to swim, and places to float rivers, etc. The site will have community pages not only talking about events happening, but need to know information for locals, i.e weather, road construction, closures, etc.
